Where do wars come from and where do you quarrel with each other? Do they not spring from your own passions that fight your members?
They covet and don't have; they kill and burn with envy but cannot obtain. They fight and make war. They do not have, because they do not ask for.
They ask and they do not receive; because they ask badly, to spend it on their pleasures.
Adulterous people! Do you not know that friendship with the world is enmity with God? Therefore, anyone who wants to be a friend to the world becomes an enemy of God.
Or do you suppose that the Scripture says in vain: The Spirit that He made dwell in us zealously longs for us?
But he gives more grace. This is why it says: God resists the proud but gives grace to the humble [a].
Submit yourselves, then, to God. Resist to Devil, and he will flee from you.
Draw close to God, and he will draw close to you. Cleanse your hands, sinners and purify your heart, you double-minded.
Grieve, mourn and cry. Her laughter turns to tears, and her joy to sadness.
Humble yourselves before the Lord, and he will exalt you.
Brothers, do not speak ill of each other. He who speaks ill of his brother or judges his brother speaks ill of the law and judges the law. And if you judge the law, then you are not a doer of the law but a judge.
There is a single Law Giver and Judge who is powerful to save and destroy. But who are you who judge your neighbor?
Let's go now those who say: "Today or tomorrow we will go to such a city, we will be there for a year and we will do business and we will win"!
You who do not know what tomorrow will be, what is your life? Because they are a vapor that appears for a little while and then vanishes.
Rather, they should say, "If the Lord wills, we will live and do this or that."
But now they boast in their pride. All boasting of this kind is bad.
Therefore, to those who know how to do good and do not do it, that is a sin.
