Do not judge, for not being judged.
For with the judgment with which you judge you will be judged, and with the measure with which you measure, you will be measured again.
And why do you look at the straw that is in your brother's eye, and do not miss the beam that is in your own eye?
Or, how will you say to your brother: Let me take the straw out of your eye, and behold the beam in your own eye?
Hypocrite! First remove the beam from your own eye, and then you will see well to remove the straw from your brother's eye.
Do not give the holy to the dogs or throw your pearls before the pigs, lest they trample them and turn and tear you apart.
Ask, and it will be given to you; seek, and you will find; knock, and it will be opened to you.
Because everyone who asks receives; and he who seeks finds; and to the caller it will be opened.
What man is there of you who, if his son asks him for bread, will give him a stone?
And if he asks for a fish, will he give him a snake?
For if you, being evil, know how to give good gifts to your children, how much more will your Father who is in heaven give good things to those who ask him?
So, all the things that you want men to do with you, so do you also with them, because this is the law and the prophets.
Enter through the narrow gate, because the gate is wide and the path that leads to perdition is spacious, and many enter through it.
For the gate is narrow and the path that leads to life narrow, and few find it.
And beware of false prophets, who come to you dressed as sheep, but inside they are ravenous wolves.
You will know them by their fruits. Are grapes picked from hawthorns or figs from thistles?
Thus, every good tree bears good fruit, but a bad tree bears bad fruit.
The good tree cannot bear bad fruit, nor the bad tree bear good fruit.
Every tree that does not bear good fruit is cut down and thrown into the fire.
So by their fruits you will know them.
Not everyone who says to me, Lord, Lord, will enter the kingdom of heaven, but he who does the will of my Father who is in heaven.
Many will say to me in that day: Lord, Lord, did we not prophesy in your name, and in your name we cast out demons and in your name we performed many miracles?
And then I will declare to them: I never knew you; depart from me, you evildoers.
Therefore, to anyone who hears these words and does them, I will compare him to a prudent man who built his house on the rock.
And the rain came down, and rivers came, and winds blew and beat that house; but it did not fall, because it was founded on the rock.
And to anyone who hears these words from me and does not do them, I will compare him to a foolish man who built his house on the sand.
And the rain came down, and rivers came, and winds blew and beat against that house; and he fell, and his ruin was great.
And it came to pass that when Jesus finished these words, the crowd was amazed at his doctrine,
because he taught them as one who has authority, and not as scribes.
